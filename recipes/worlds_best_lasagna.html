World's Best Lasagna

Dish Description

One of our top-performing recipes of all time, World's Best Lasagna racks up more than 7 million views per year and has ranked among the most popular lasagna recipes on the internet for two decades. Make our most popular lasagna today to find out what all the fuss is about!

Ingredients

  • Sweet italian sausage
  • Lean ground beef
  • One onion
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • One can crushed tomatoes
  • Two cans tomato sauce
  • Two cans tomato paste
  • Two tablespoons white sugar
  • Fresh parsley
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • One egg

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna:
    • Meat sauce
    • Noodles
    • Ricotta mixture
    • Mozzarella slices
    • Meat sauce
    • Parmesan cheese
    • Repeat the layers, and top with parmasan
  5. Cover with foil and bake.

The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F. Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes. Also, it's important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
